How to Play
  1. Set your bet amount.
  2. Bet types:
    • Winner Only ‚Äî predict which Ace finishes first.

  4. Flip cards from the deck ‚Äî each card advances the ace of the same suit.
  5. Return to Player (RTP): 96%
